Directions

Starting in Jackson Wisconsin, get on US-45. After three minutes, take I-42, I-94E, I-294 S to 14th Ave N in Nashville. This part of the drive is a little less than nine hours, but it is a straight shot to Nashville. Then drive to Church Street and you have made it to Nashville. This route will take you through Chicago and Louisville where there could be a decent amount of traffic. The amount of time it takes to get there varies because of construction and traffic.
